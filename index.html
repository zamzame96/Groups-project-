Rubber Plant

Care Tips:

  • Light: Bright, indirect sunlight.
  • Water: Water when the top inch of soil is dry.
  • Temperature: Prefers temperatures between 60-75°F (15-24°C).
  • Humidity: Enjoys high humidity but can adapt to average indoor humidity.
  • Soil: Well-draining potting mix.

Chinese Evergreen Crete

care Tips
  • I'm a compact plant and a relatively slow grower but I could reach as high as 1m.
  • I like medium to moderate light. If my leaves are dark, they can handle more light, if they're light, I'll only be able to tolerate medium light.
  • Keep my soil lightly moist constantly but be careful not to overwater me as my delicate roots are susceptible to rot.
  • Keep me away from draughts and in temperatures of between 18 and 24°C.
  • Please feed me diluted fertiliser once a month during the growing season.

Aloe plants care Tips
  • Light: Aloe plants need bright, indirect sunlight. They can tolerate some direct sunlight, especially in the morning, but too much direct sun can cause the leaves to turn brown.
  • Water: Water deeply but infrequently. Allow the top inch or two of soil to dry out completely between waterings. Overwatering can lead to root rot.
  • Temperature: Aloe plants prefer temperatures between 55-80°F (13-27°C). They can tolerate cooler temperatures but should be protected from frost.
  • Humidity: Aloe plants do well in low humidity. They are adapted to arid conditions and do not require additional humidity.
  • Soil: Use a well-draining soil mix, such as a cactus or succulent mix. Adding perlite or sand to regular potting soil can improve drainage.
Orchids care Tips
  • Light: Orchids prefer bright, indirect light. Direct sunlight can scorch the leaves, so place them in a location where they receive filtered light or morning sun.
  • Water: Water orchids once a week, allowing the potting medium to dry out slightly between waterings. Avoid letting the plant sit in water, as this can lead to root rot.
  • Temperature: Orchids thrive in temperatures between 60-75°F (16-24°C) during the day and slightly cooler at night. Avoid exposing them to cold drafts or sudden temperature changes.
  • Humidity: Orchids prefer high humidity levels, ideally between 50-70%. Increase humidity by placing a tray of water with pebbles under the pot or using a humidifier.
  • Soil: Use a well-draining orchid mix, typically made of bark, perlite, and sphagnum moss. Avoid using regular potting soil as it retains too much moisture.
